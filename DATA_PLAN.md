# Data Collection Plan

## Objective

Collect publicly available datasets that will support analysis of Chicago's live music and entertainment market.

## Required Datasets

| Dataset                    | Purpose                                                | Status          |
| -------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ | --------------- |
| Chicago music venues       | Identify venue locations and neighborhood distribution | ⏳ Not Collected |
| Concert and event listings | Analyze seasonality and event trends                   | ⏳ Not Collected |
| Artist information         | Analyze genres and artist popularity                   | ⏳ Not Collected |
| Chicago demographic data   | Provide marketing context for recommendations          | ⏳ Not Collected |

## Notes

Each dataset should directly support one or more business questions. Only publicly available data will be used, and all sources will be documented in the final report.
