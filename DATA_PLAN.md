# Data Collection Plan

## Objective

Collect publicly available datasets that support analysis of Chicago's live music and entertainment market.

The purpose of this project is to understand venue opportunities, audience demographics, music trends, and artist characteristics that may influence audience engagement.

---

## Dataset Plan

| Dataset | Purpose | Status |
|---|---|---|
| Chicago Music & Dance Venues | Identify live music venue locations and analyze geographic distribution across Chicago | ✅ Collected |
| Chicago Community Demographic Data (ACS) | Provide audience context through population, age, and income analysis | ✅ Collected |
| Lollapalooza Performers (2005–2025) | Analyze Chicago music trends, artist popularity, and genre patterns over time | ✅ Collected |
| Spotify Tracks Dataset | Analyze relationships between music characteristics and track popularity | ✅ Collected |

---

## Dataset Selection Criteria

Each dataset should directly support one or more business questions.

Datasets were selected based on their ability to provide insights into:

- Geographic opportunities for music marketing
- Potential audience demographics
- Music genre and artist trends
- Factors associated with popularity and engagement

Only publicly available datasets are being used, and sources will be documented throughout the project.

---

## Next Steps

1. Explore and understand each dataset
2. Clean and prepare data for analysis
3. Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
4. Create visualizations and dashboards
5. Develop data-driven marketing recommendations
